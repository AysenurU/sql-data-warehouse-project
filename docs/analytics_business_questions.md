# Analytics Business Questions

This document lists the core business questions supported by the Gold analytics layer.

## Sales Performance
- How much revenue do we generate daily and monthly?
  - gold.daily_sales
  - gold.monthly_sales

- What are the top-performing products by revenue?
  - gold.top_products
  - gold.product_sales

## Customer Analytics
- Who are our most valuable customers?
  - gold.customer_metrics

- How does customer purchasing behavior change over time?
  - gold.customer_sales

- How many new vs returning customers do we have per day?
  - gold.new_vs_returning_customers

## Executive KPIs
- What is the overall business performance?
  - gold.sales_kpis

- Which product categories drive the most revenue?
  - gold.sales_by_category
